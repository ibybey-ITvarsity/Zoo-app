WARTHOGS

Phacochoerus is a genus in the family Suidae, commonly known as warthogs (pronounced wart-hog)

It is the sole genus of subfamily Phacochoerinae.

These pigs live in open and semiopen habitats, even in quite arid regions, in sub-Saharan Africa.
